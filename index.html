About the Project

The Gender Family is a community driven project consisting of 4999 hand-drawn and unique NFT artworks living on the Ethereum blockchain. Our Project is a culture shift and all characters in the collection are gender neutral, which makes it the first collection that represents the LGBT+ movement. Through the Gender Family, art and fashion lovers can own a unique piece of art while making the Web3 space more diverse and attractive.

Mission

To be the biggest family in Web3.

Together, we will bring the Diversity Movement to the NFT Space and create equal opportunities for all. It's time to make an impact around the globe!

Our Community

As a community that is held together by the artwork in TGF's collection, we are dedicated to create an inclusive and welcoming future for all. The family consists of people from all over the world, with a wide range of cultures, backgrounds and more.
